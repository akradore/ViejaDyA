POS Logo on Session and Receipt

If you are searching for an app which will help you to print your logo in POS receipt with company's information then this app is an end to your search. This app is useful to show configured Logo on the POS session as well as shows your logo in POS receipt.

Features

Add logo to POS

You can add POS logo in Point Of Sale configuration and logo is displayed in POS screen.

Logo in POS receipt

Logo is also displayed in POS payment receipt.
